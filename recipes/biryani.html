Biryani

Home page

Description

Biryani is a popular Indian dish made with rice, meat, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken or lamb
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and fry the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the meat and cook until browned.
  4. Add the tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, cilantro, and mint leaves. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Add water and salt to taste.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and the flavors are well combined.
