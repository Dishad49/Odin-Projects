Taco

Home page

Description

A delicious Mexican dish made with a soft tortilla filled with seasoned meat, vegetables, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and mix well.
  3. Warm the tortillas according to package instructions.
  4. Fill each tortilla with the seasoned meat and desired toppings.
  5. Serve with sour cream and salsa.
