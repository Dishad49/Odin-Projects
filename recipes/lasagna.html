Lasagna

Home page

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 10 oz lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the ricotta cheese and mix well.
  5. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
